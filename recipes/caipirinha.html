Caipirinha Cocktail

Description:

The caipirinha is an alcoholic cocktail of Brazilian origin (São Paulo), made with cachaça, sugar, lemon and ice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lime, quartered
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 fluid ounces (120ml) of cachaça (Brazilian rum)
  • 1 ½ cups ice

Instructions:

  1. Combine sugar and lime quarters in a pint glass.
  2. Crush and mix together using a cocktail muddler.
  3. Add cachaça and stir. Fill with ice and stir again.
