Manhattan

Description:

A classic cocktail that is believed to date back to the late 1800s. Is a cocktail made with whiskey, sweet vermouth and bitters.

Ingredients:

  • 2 fluid ounces rye whiskey
  • Â½ fluid ounce sweet vermouth
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 maraschino cherry

Instructions:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine whiskey, vermouth, and bitters in a cocktail mixing glass. Add ice and stir until chilled.
  3. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass and garnish with a maraschino cherry.
