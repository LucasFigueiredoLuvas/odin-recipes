Cuba Libre

Description:

Cuba-libre is an alcoholic drink or cocktail made with rum, cola and lime.

Ingredients:

  • 4 ice cubes
  • 2 fluid ounces (120ml) rum
  • 4 fluid ounces (240ml) cola
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice, or to taste

Instructions:

  1. Fill a 12-ounce glass with ice cubes.
  2. Pour rum and cola into the glass.
  3. Stir in lime juice and serve.
